Top 10 Juventus Legends

Like every major successful European club, Serie A's Juventus are a club rich with history. They've won a record 27 league titles, nine Italian Cups, four Italian Supercups, and eleven internaitonal titles (two Intercontinental Cups, two European Champion Clubs' Cup/UEFA Champions Leagues, one European Cup Winners' Cup, a record three UEFA Cups, one UEFA Intertoto Cup and two UEFA Super Cups). That's a total of 51 titles, making them the most success club in Italy and the seventh most successful in the world in terms of the number of trophies won by the club. With rich history, comes top-class legends. Legends that paved the way to success, and willed their team to victory. There are at least a couple of them in the current Juventus side, which looks set to secure at least one piece of silverware by the end of this season. Since Juventus' rich history contains so many world-class legends that all deserve respect and recognition, I've decided to limit this list to the legends we've had the privilege of witnessing over the past two decades.
